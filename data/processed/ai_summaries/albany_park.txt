# Albany Park Neighborhood Profile

With 1,395 active business licenses, Albany Park maintains a robust commercial presence dominated by food and beverage establishments alongside retail and miscellaneous services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.08 indicates a relatively concentrated economy, suggesting these top sectors significantly outpace other business categories. Community sentiment remains positive overall, and online discussions frequently reference local parks and recreational spaces, including mentions of the park district and nearby attractions, alongside practical concerns about parking availability.
