# Albany Park Neighborhood Profile

With 1,395 active business licenses concentrated in food & beverage, retail, and miscellaneous services, Albany Park supports a moderately diverse commercial ecosystem (Shannon entropy: 1.08). The 82 Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $102 indicate modest tourist activity, while Reddit discussions reveal residents' strong connection to local amenities like Foundry Park and the park district. Notably, visitors emphasize proximity to transit—particularly the Brown Line—and highlight convenient access to nearby neighborhoods like Lincoln Square, whereas residents focus more on neighborhood institutions and practical concerns like parking availability.
