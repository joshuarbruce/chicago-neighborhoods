# Armour Square Neighborhood Profile

With 526 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial ecosystem anchored primarily by food and beverage establishments alongside other services and health-focused businesses. The business diversity index of 1.19 indicates a relatively concentrated commercial landscape, suggesting these core sectors dominate local economic activity. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood is positive, and online discussions reflect engagement with nearby attractions like Millennium Park, the Chicago River water taxi, and adjacent neighborhoods such as Logan Square and Wicker Park, positioning Armour Square within a broader regional tourism and dining destination context.
