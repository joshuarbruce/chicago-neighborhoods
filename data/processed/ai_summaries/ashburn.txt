# Ashburn, Chicago

With 673 active business licenses across diverse sectors led by food and beverage operations, Ashburn supports a robust local commercial ecosystem. Residents engage in discussions centered on transportation connections and comparisons to neighboring Washington Heights and Morgan Park, suggesting commuting patterns and regional identity are central to their neighborhood experience. Visitors, by contrast, focus heavily on food—particularly deep dish pizza—and the novelty of compact accommodations like tiny homes and suites, indicating that tourists value the neighborhood's culinary offerings and unique lodging options in ways that don't prominently feature in resident conversations.
