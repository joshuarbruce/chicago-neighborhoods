# Avalon Park Neighborhood Profile

With 212 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial base anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1 suggests a relatively concentrated economy with limited variety across sectors. Community discussions highlight civic infrastructure and recreational amenities, particularly the park district and river system, while sentiment toward the neighborhood remains positive. The commercial landscape reflects a neighborhood oriented toward local services and dining options rather than specialized or niche businesses.
