# Avondale Neighborhood Profile

With 1,097 active business licenses and a modest business diversity index of 1.16, Avondale's economy is dominated by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. Residents highlight the neighborhood's affordability and walkability, with particular enthusiasm for recreational amenities like climbing gyms and nearby natural spaces, while visitors gravitate toward its transit accessibility via the Blue Line and proximity to coffee shops and trendy nearby areas like Logan Square. The neighborhood maintains positive community sentiment overall, though Airbnb guests appear to emphasize convenient transportation links and dining culture—aspects less central to resident conversations, which focus more on outdoor recreation and neighborhood character. At a median nightly rate of $118 across 202 active listings, Avondale attracts a moderate short-term rental market.
