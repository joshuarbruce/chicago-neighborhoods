# Belmont Cragin

With nearly 2,000 active business licenses, this neighborhood supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.16 indicates moderate specialization, suggesting the economy leans toward particular sectors rather than spreading evenly across industries. Community discussions highlight local landmarks and transit access, particularly references to the Belmont train stop and Brown/Red/Purple lines, pointing to public transportation as a significant neighborhood feature. Overall community sentiment registers as positive, reflecting generally favorable perceptions among residents and online commenters.
