# Beverly Neighborhood Profile

With 454 active business licenses, Beverly supports a modest commercial ecosystem anchored primarily by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous service businesses. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.01 indicates relatively low economic diversification, suggesting a concentration in a limited number of sectors. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood is positive, and online discussions frequently reference local parks, cultural venues, and community gathering spaces, indicating these amenities play a meaningful role in residents' engagement with the area.
