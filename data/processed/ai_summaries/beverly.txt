# Beverly Neighborhood Profile

With 454 active business licenses concentrated primarily in food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Beverly supports a modest commercial ecosystem with relatively low business diversity. The neighborhood maintains positive community sentiment, with residents actively discussing local landmarks like Mount Greenwood Park, Beverly Cemetery, and community gathering spaces that serve as central hubs. The modest Airbnb market—21 listings at a median nightly rate of $105—suggests limited tourism infrastructure, though visitor reviews highlight specific local establishments and proximity to transit options like the Rock Island Line. While residents emphasize community spaces and local institutions, Airbnb guests appear to value proximity to specific restaurants and accessibility to transportation networks, suggesting visitors prioritize convenience and dining experiences over the neighborhood amenities that define resident life.
