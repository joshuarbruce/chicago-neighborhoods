# Bridgeport

With 675 active business licenses concentrated primarily in food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Bridgeport supports a moderately diverse local economy (diversity index: 1.25). The neighborhood hosts 230 Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $68, suggesting accessibility for budget-conscious visitors drawn to cultural landmarks like the White Sox stadium and the Bridgeport Art Center. While residents engage in discussions around practical neighborhood services—automotive work, social services, and local connections to adjacent Pilsen—visitors appear more focused on the neighborhood's cultural attractions and coffee shop scene. Overall community sentiment registers as positive across both resident and visitor perspectives.
