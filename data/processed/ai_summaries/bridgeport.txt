# Bridgeport Neighborhood Profile

With 675 active business licenses, Bridgeport maintains a modest commercial base dominated by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.25 suggests a moderately concentrated economy, with less variety compared to more economically diversified areas. Community discussions reflect engagement around practical services—particularly automotive repair—alongside social services and education-related topics, indicating resident concerns around both local commerce and community support systems. Overall community sentiment toward the neighborhood registers as positive, despite the relatively specialized nature of its business landscape.
