# Brighton Park Neighborhood Profile

Nearly 1,000 active businesses anchor this neighborhood, with food and beverage establishments leading alongside automotive and transportation services, though the overall business diversity remains relatively concentrated. Residents engage most frequently with local landmarks like Big Star and Los Gallos, alongside discussions of park district amenities and the river system, while Airbnb visitors emphasize convenient transit access via the Orange Line and Kedzie station. The 37 active short-term rental listings at a median nightly rate of $88 suggest moderate tourist traffic, with guest reviews notably highlighting responsive hosts rather than neighborhood attractions—a contrast to residents' focus on local institutions and gathering spaces. Community sentiment trends positive overall, supported by a robust small business ecosystem.
