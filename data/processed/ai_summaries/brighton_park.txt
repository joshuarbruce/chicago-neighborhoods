# Brighton Park Neighborhood Profile

With 981 active business licenses, Brighton Park maintains a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.09 indicates moderate economic specialization, suggesting the neighborhood economy relies on a relatively concentrated set of industries rather than a broad mix. Community sentiment in the neighborhood registers as positive, reflecting residents' favorable outlook on their area. Online discussions among residents frequently reference local institutions and recreational amenities, including the park district and river system, alongside nearby neighborhoods and dining destinations like Los Gallos.
