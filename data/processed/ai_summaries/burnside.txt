# Burnside

With 32 active business licenses concentrated in food & beverage, automotive, and other services, Burnside supports a modest commercial footprint with moderate business diversity. Residents engage enthusiastically with the neighborhood's transit infrastructure, particularly the commuter rail connections and what they describe as clean, bold design elements in local maps and geography. The three active Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $151 attract international visitors who emphasize hospitality and accommodations, suggesting that while residents value transit access and design aesthetics, short-term guests prioritize friendly service and practical amenities. Overall community sentiment remains positive despite the neighborhood's relatively small visitor economy.
