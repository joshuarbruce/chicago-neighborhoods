# Calumet Heights Neighborhood Profile

With 210 active business licenses, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial presence concentrated primarily in food and beverage, automotive and transportation, and miscellaneous services. The business diversity index of 1.11 indicates a relatively narrow economic base with limited variety across business sectors. Community sentiment remains positive overall, though Reddit discussions reveal significant local focus on regional infrastructure projects, sports developments, and tax policy concerns. The concentration of conversation around topics like the bears stadium and river systems suggests infrastructure and development are central to neighborhood discourse.
