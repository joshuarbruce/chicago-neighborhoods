# Chatham Neighborhood Profile

With 648 active business licenses, Chatham maintains a modest commercial presence anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside retail and miscellaneous services. The business diversity index of 1.09 indicates a relatively concentrated economy focused on a few dominant sectors rather than a wide variety of business types. Despite positive overall community sentiment, online discussions reveal tensions around neighborhood perception, with residents noting both appreciation for maintained spaces and defensiveness about external characterizations. Community conversations suggest strong local identity among long-term residents, who distinguish themselves from newer arrivals while discussing conditions in adjacent neighborhoods like Auburn Gresham.
