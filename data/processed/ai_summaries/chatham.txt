# Chatham Neighborhood Profile

With 648 active business licenses concentrated in food and beverage, retail, and miscellaneous services, Chatham supports a moderately diverse local economy (diversity index: 1.09). Residents discussing the neighborhood on Reddit emphasize its authentic character and long-standing community ties, frequently referencing native Chicagoans and established gathering places, though crime concerns surface in some conversations. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, highlight the warmth of hosts and the comfort of accommodations—praising personal touches and hospitable service in ways that rarely appear in resident discussions, suggesting that visitor experiences center on hospitality and relaxation rather than neighborhood identity. Overall community sentiment registers as positive across platforms.
