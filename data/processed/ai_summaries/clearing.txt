# Clearing Neighborhood Profile

With 318 active business licenses and moderate diversity (0.84 Shannon entropy), Clearing supports a varied economy anchored in automotive, transportation, and food-and-beverage services. Residents engage primarily with practical concerns—infrastructure costs like tolls, public services, water quality testing, and financial hardship—reflecting lived economic pressures in the neighborhood. The small Airbnb market (12 listings at a $167 median nightly rate) emphasizes basic amenities like cleanliness and functionality, suggesting visitors prioritize practical comfort over distinctive local experiences, while residents' discussions reveal deeper concerns about poverty, emergency response, and environmental health that tourists do not encounter.
