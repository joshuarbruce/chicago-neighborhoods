# Dunning

With 706 active business licenses across diverse sectors—led by food and beverage, health and personal care, and other services—Dunning supports a modest commercial ecosystem reflecting moderate business diversity. Residents engage enthusiastically with transit infrastructure, particularly the MTA commuter rail connections, and express appreciation for well-designed maps and geographic resources that help them navigate the neighborhood. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, emphasize personalized hospitality experiences and quirky aesthetic details—themed spaces, responsive hosts, and distinctive design elements—suggesting that transient guests value the neighborhood's character and individual rental experiences more than the practical transit connectivity that draws resident interest. Overall community sentiment remains positive across both groups.
