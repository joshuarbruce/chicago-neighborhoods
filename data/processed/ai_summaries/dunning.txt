# Dunning Neighborhood Profile

With 706 active business licenses, Dunning supports a modest commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.18 suggests a relatively concentrated commercial base, with limited variety beyond its dominant sectors. Community sentiment toward the area remains positive, with residents expressing particular enthusiasm for transit infrastructure and design aesthetics, frequently discussing commuter rail connectivity and appreciation for clean, bold cartographic design. This combination of practical transit access and design-forward community values characterizes local priorities and identity.
