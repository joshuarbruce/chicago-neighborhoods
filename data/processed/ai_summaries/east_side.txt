# East Side Neighborhood Profile

With 264 active businesses dominated by automotive, transportation, and food and beverage services, East Side maintains a relatively concentrated economic base (diversity index: 1.04). Residents engage actively in civic life, with neighborhood discourse centering on elections, voting resources, and community mutual aid—concerns that rarely surface in visitor reviews. The 15 active Airbnb listings, priced at a median of $42 nightly, suggest East Side functions primarily as a residential neighborhood rather than a tourist destination. Overall community sentiment remains positive, though visitors and residents occupy distinctly different conversational spaces: while residents discuss civic participation and public services, guests focus narrowly on specific accommodations and host interactions.
