# Edgewater

With 1,255 active business licenses and a moderate diversity index, Edgewater supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage, health and personal care services, and miscellaneous retail. Residents engage actively with neighborhood logistics—discussing seasonal concerns like snow removal and ground-floor retail spaces—while maintaining strong community ties to adjacent Andersonville and hosting regular events like fall markets. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, emphasize proximity to transit and institutions like Loyola University, alongside leisure activities such as coffee shops and cycling, suggesting that tourists and short-term visitors value accessibility and recreational amenities more than the infrastructure and local event planning that dominate resident conversations. Overall community sentiment registers as positive across both groups.
