# Edison Park Neighborhood Profile

With 276 active business licenses, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial footprint anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The business diversity index of 1.4 suggests a relatively concentrated economic base rather than a wide range of business types. Community members express positive sentiment overall, and local conversations frequently reference recreational amenities like the park district and river system, along with neighboring areas and local venues. The neighborhood's commercial and social character reflects a community-oriented environment with particular strength in service-oriented businesses.
