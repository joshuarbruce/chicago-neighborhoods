# Edison Park Neighborhood Profile

With 276 active business licenses concentrated primarily in food & beverage, health & personal care, and miscellaneous services, Edison Park supports a modest commercial landscape with limited business diversity (1.4 Shannon entropy score). Residents engage actively with local institutions like the park district and river system, while also discussing regional connections to areas like Arlington Heights and Lincoln Park. Visitors, though few in number (3 Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $70), highlight the neighborhood's library and proximity to nearby amenities and entertainment options—aspects that appear less prominent in resident conversations. Overall community sentiment remains positive across both visitor and resident perspectives.
