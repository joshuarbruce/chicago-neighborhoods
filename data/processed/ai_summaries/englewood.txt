# Englewood Neighborhood Profile

With 402 active business licenses and a business diversity index of 1.14, Englewood supports a modest commercial ecosystem anchored primarily in food and beverage, retail, and miscellaneous services. Residents highlight the neighborhood's affordability, accessibility to public transit via the Red Line, and proximity to Kennedy-King College, while also discussing rental prices and white-collar employment opportunities. The 61 active Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $44 suggest the neighborhood attracts budget-conscious visitors, who notably emphasize personal service and property maintenance in their reviews—aspects that appear less central to resident discussions, which focus more on institutional access and housing costs. Overall community sentiment registers as positive across both resident and visitor perspectives.
