# Englewood Neighborhood Profile

With 402 active business licenses anchored primarily in food and beverage, retail, and miscellaneous services, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial foundation. The business diversity index of 1.14 suggests a relatively concentrated economic landscape, with limited variety in the types of enterprises operating in the area. Community discussions highlight affordability concerns around rental and housing prices, alongside appreciation for accessibility to public transit and cultural attractions near Kennedy King College. Overall sentiment toward the neighborhood registers as positive, with residents and visitors engaged in conversations about coffee shops, residential opportunities, and the area's academic resources.
