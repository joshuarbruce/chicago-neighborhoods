# Forest Glen Neighborhood Profile

With 340 active business licenses concentrated in food and beverage, health care, and miscellaneous services, Forest Glen maintains a modest commercial presence with moderate business diversity. The neighborhood generates positive community sentiment, with residents highlighting local amenities like the forest preserves, Northwestern Medicine facilities, and praising the area's natural beauty as "absolutely stunning." The limited Airbnb market—just eight active listings at a median nightly rate of $106—suggests Forest Glen remains primarily residential rather than a visitor destination. Notably, Airbnb reviews emphasize personalized hospitality experiences with specific hosts, whereas residents tend to focus on public infrastructure, natural features, and institutional anchors rather than the neighborhood's visitor appeal.
