# Forest Glen Neighborhood Profile

With 340 active business licenses, Forest Glen supports a modest commercial base anchored primarily by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 0.81 suggests a moderately concentrated economy, with these core categories dominating the commercial landscape. Online discussions highlight community engagement around local infrastructure—particularly traffic safety concerns related to stop signs—and appreciation for the forest preserves, while mentions of Northwestern Medicine indicate the presence of significant medical institutions. Overall sentiment from community members skews positive, with descriptors like "absolutely stunning" and "amazing poetic" reflecting resident satisfaction with the neighborhood's character and amenities.
