# Fuller Park Neighborhood Profile

With 115 active business licenses, Fuller Park supports a modest commercial base anchored by food and beverage, retail, and miscellaneous services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.16 suggests a relatively concentrated economy with limited variety across sectors. Community sentiment toward the area registers as positive overall. Online discussions about the neighborhood frequently reference nearby attractions and institutions like Grant Park and the park district, alongside mentions of local businesses and community events like birthday parties.
