# Fuller Park Neighborhood Profile

With 115 active business licenses dominated by food and beverage and retail operations, Fuller Park supports a modest commercial ecosystem showing moderate business diversity. Residents engage most frequently in discussions about local parks, the park district, and river systems, suggesting these public spaces anchor community life, while also referencing nearby landmarks like Lincoln Park and Grant Park. The neighborhood's small Airbnb market (6 active listings at a $150 median nightly rate) attracts visitors who emphasize house rules and proximity to White Sox Stadium—practical and sports-focused concerns that differ notably from residents' emphasis on parks and recreational infrastructure. Overall community sentiment toward the neighborhood registers as positive.
