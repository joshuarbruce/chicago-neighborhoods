# Gage Park Neighborhood Profile

With 635 active business licenses concentrated heavily in food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Gage Park supports a modest commercial ecosystem that shows limited diversification. Residents engage most actively in conversations about local parks and recreational spaces, suggesting these amenities play a meaningful role in community life, while also discussing connections to nearby neighborhoods like Arlington Heights and Lincoln Park. The sparse Airbnb market (6 active listings at a median of $86 per night) reveals that visitors are primarily concerned with practical accommodation matters—hot water, cleanliness, and basic functionality—rather than the neighborhood attractions and community spaces that dominate resident conversations. Overall community sentiment remains positive despite a low business diversity index of 1.11, indicating a tightly-focused local economy centered on essential services.
