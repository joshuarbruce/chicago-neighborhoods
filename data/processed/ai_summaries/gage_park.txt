# Gage Park Neighborhood Profile

With 635 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a diverse commercial landscape anchored primarily by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.11 indicates a moderately concentrated economy, suggesting that while these three primary sectors dominate, supporting businesses contribute to the overall commercial ecosystem. Community sentiment remains positive, reflecting residents' engagement with local amenities and services. Online discussions highlight connections to broader Chicago institutions like the park district and river system, alongside hyperlocal references like Big Star restaurant, indicating an active community invested in both neighborhood-specific and citywide affairs.
