# Garfield Ridge

With 606 active business licenses anchored primarily in food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Garfield Ridge maintains a modest commercial ecosystem that residents engage with regularly, particularly around transit nodes like the Metra station and Northwestern Medicine facilities. Reddit discussions reflect a community oriented toward local civic life—earth day initiatives, holiday traditions, and practical neighborhood infrastructure like bike racks—suggesting residents value sustainability and connectivity. Airbnb visitors, despite limited short-term rental activity (12 listings), emphasize proximity to Midway Airport and highlight personalized host interactions, indicating that travelers view the neighborhood primarily as a convenient base rather than a destination in itself, a distinction residents' own conversations don't reflect.
