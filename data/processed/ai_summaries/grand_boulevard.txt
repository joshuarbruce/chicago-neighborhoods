# Grand Boulevard Neighborhood Profile

With 373 active business licenses, Grand Boulevard maintains a modest commercial presence anchored primarily by food and beverage establishments, automotive services, and miscellaneous retail. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.15 indicates a relatively concentrated economy with limited variety across business sectors. Online discussions reflect an optimistic outlook, with residents and prospective newcomers expressing enthusiasm about the area's potential and transit connections. Community conversations suggest active engagement around neighborhood development, transit accessibility, and housing prospects.
