# Grand Boulevard

With 373 active business licenses and a business diversity index of 1.15, Grand Boulevard maintains a modest commercial presence anchored by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services. The neighborhood's positive community sentiment is reflected in both resident and visitor engagement, though the two groups emphasize different aspects: residents discuss practical concerns like transit connectivity and neighborhood planning, while the 165 active Airbnb listings (median $144/night) suggest visitors are drawn to the area's historic character and leisure amenities like recreation spaces. Visitors frequently describe Grand Boulevard through the lens of its Bronzeville heritage and positioning as a hidden oasis, language that emphasizes discovery and charm rather than the infrastructure and development themes that dominate resident conversations.
