# Greater Grand Crossing Neighborhood Profile

With 591 active business licenses, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial presence anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.16 indicates a relatively concentrated economic base, suggesting these few dominant sectors represent a significant share of local commercial activity. Community sentiment registers as positive overall, though Reddit discussions reveal substantial civic engagement focused on policing accountability, consent decrees, and interactions with public agencies. This mix of stable business activity and active community dialogue around governance reflects a neighborhood actively monitoring and discussing its public institutions.
