# Hegewisch Neighborhood Profile

With 133 active business licenses concentrated in food & beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Hegewisch supports a modest commercial base that reflects lower business diversity. Resident discussions emphasize affordable living, casual social activities like attending Bears games, and access to breakfast spots, suggesting a neighborhood valued by locals for its budget-friendly character and neighborhood gathering places. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, prioritize accessibility features—wheelchair access, changing tables, and walkable proximity to transit and amenities—indicating that the neighborhood's appeal to short-term visitors centers on practical inclusivity rather than the laid-back, economical lifestyle that residents highlight. The positive community sentiment suggests residents and visitors alike find value in Hegewisch, though they prioritize distinctly different neighborhood qualities.
