# Hegewisch Neighborhood Profile

With 133 active business licenses, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial presence anchored by food and beverage establishments, automotive services, and miscellaneous other businesses. The business diversity index of 1.12 suggests a relatively concentrated economic base with limited variety across business types. Community sentiment remains positive, while online discussions highlight residents' interests in local dining options, recreational activities like Bears games, and creative pursuits, reflecting an engaged population drawn to both budget-friendly amenities and off-the-beaten-path experiences.
