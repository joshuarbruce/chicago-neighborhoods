# Humboldt Park Neighborhood Profile

With 1,255 active business licenses and a business diversity index of 1.17, Humboldt Park supports a moderately varied commercial landscape anchored by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous service providers. Residents engage most actively with the neighborhood's parks and transit infrastructure, frequently discussing the park district, pace buses, and the area's Ukrainian Village character near the Wicker Park border. Visitors booking the 120 active Airbnb listings (median $106/night) emphasize amenities like hot tubs and event spaces alongside proximity to nearby Logan Square and the Green Line, suggesting tourists prioritize leisure accommodations and transit access more than residents, who focus on community institutions and cultural identity. The neighborhood maintains overall positive community sentiment despite moderate business diversity.
