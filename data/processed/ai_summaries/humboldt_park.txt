# Humboldt Park Neighborhood Summary

With 1,255 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial landscape anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.17 indicates a relatively concentrated economic base, suggesting that a few business categories dominate the local commercial activity rather than a broad range of enterprises. Community sentiment toward the area remains positive, and online discussions frequently reference the neighborhood's parks, public transit options via buses, and cultural institutions like Big Star, while also highlighting its proximity to Ukrainian Village and Wicker Park. The mention of wildlife like bald eagles alongside recreational activities such as birthday parties and park district programming suggests residents value the neighborhood's natural amenities and community gathering spaces.
