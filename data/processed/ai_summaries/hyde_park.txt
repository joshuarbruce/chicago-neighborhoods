# Hyde Park Neighborhood Profile

With 420 active business licenses, Hyde Park supports a modest commercial landscape anchored by food and beverage, health and personal care, and miscellaneous other services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.09 suggests a relatively concentrated economic base rather than a highly varied commercial ecosystem. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood is positive, and local discussions frequently reference campus spaces, recreational amenities like Divvy bikes and parks, and nearby landmarks such as Harper Court and the river system. This combination of focused commercial activity, positive community perception, and engagement with shared public spaces characterizes the neighborhood's current profile.
