# Hyde Park Neighborhood Summary

With 420 active business licenses concentrated in food and beverage, health, and personal care sectors, Hyde Park supports a moderately diverse local economy. The neighborhood attracts substantial tourism around the University of Chicago campus, evident in 125 active Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $108, where visitors frequently highlight lakefront attractions like Promontory Point and campus-adjacent amenities. While Airbnb guests emphasize scenic destinations and proximity to the university, residents engage more with local infrastructure—discussing Divvy bikes, the park district, and the river system—suggesting that day-to-day community life centers on accessibility and recreational assets beyond campus-oriented tourism. The neighborhood maintains overall positive community sentiment across both visitor and resident discussions.
