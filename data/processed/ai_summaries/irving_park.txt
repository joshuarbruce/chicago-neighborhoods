# Irving Park Neighborhood Profile

With 1,662 active business licenses and a moderate business diversity index of 1.18, Irving Park supports a robust local economy anchored primarily by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services. Residents' Reddit discussions emphasize community-oriented amenities like the park district, local businesses such as Big Star, and neighborhood events like spring yard sales, reflecting a tight-knit residential character. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, focus heavily on transit connectivity—particularly the Blue Line and its various stations—suggesting that accessible public transportation and proximity to adjacent neighborhoods like Logan Square are key draws for short-term guests willing to pay a median nightly rate of $126. The neighborhood's positive overall community sentiment aligns with residents' emphasis on local institutions and services, even as visitors appear primarily attracted by Irving Park's role as a transit-accessible base for exploring the broader area.
