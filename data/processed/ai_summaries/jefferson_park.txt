# Jefferson Park, Chicago

With 533 active business licenses anchored primarily in food and beverage, automotive, and other services, Jefferson Park supports a moderately diverse local economy (Shannon entropy: 1.21). Residents engage most frequently with community institutions like the park district and nearby attractions such as the river system and Big Star, while celebrating neighborhood traditions like the holiday train. Visitors booking the 54 active Airbnb listings (median $109/night) emphasize transit convenience—particularly the Blue Line and Jefferson Park station—and cultural venues like the Copernicus Center, suggesting that easy access to transportation and specific attractions may matter more to short-term guests than to long-term residents. Overall community sentiment remains positive across both groups.
