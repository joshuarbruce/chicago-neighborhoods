# Kenwood Neighborhood Profile

With 186 active business licenses, Kenwood maintains a modest commercial footprint anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 0.88 indicates a relatively concentrated economy, suggesting these core sectors dominate the local commercial landscape. Community discussions emphasize the area's strong schools, proximity to Washington Park, and reputation for safety and diversity, while residents actively engage in social clubs and community organizations. Overall sentiment toward the neighborhood remains positive, reflecting satisfaction with educational institutions and the residential environment.
