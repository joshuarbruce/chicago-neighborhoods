# Kenwood Neighborhood Profile

With 186 active business licenses and a moderate diversity index of 0.88, Kenwood supports a varied economic base anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. Residents emphasize the neighborhood's strong schools, proximity to Washington Park, and welcoming community clubs and social spaces, while consistently describing the area as safe and diverse. Airbnb visitors, staying at a median nightly rate of $89 across 82 listings, appear notably drawn to personalized hospitality experiences—frequently mentioning homemade baked goods and specific hosts by name—a dimension of neighborhood character that doesn't emerge prominently in resident discussions.
