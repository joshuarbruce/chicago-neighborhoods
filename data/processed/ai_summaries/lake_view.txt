# Lake View Neighborhood Profile

With 3,366 active business licenses and a median Airbnb nightly rate of $170, Lake View supports a substantial commercial ecosystem dominated by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. Residents emphasize the neighborhood's social scene and weekend activities, frequently discussing local venues like Dryhop Brewers and Music Box, while visitors are notably more focused on proximity to Wrigley Field and the Cubs, as well as transit access and dining options along the Southport Corridor. This suggests that while residents value Lake View as a community gathering space, visitors are drawn primarily by its role as a sports and entertainment destination with strong public transportation connections. The neighborhood's positive community sentiment reflects an active, engaged population despite the relatively modest business diversity index of 1.25.
