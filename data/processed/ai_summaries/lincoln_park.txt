# Lincoln Park

With 2,127 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The business diversity index of 1.25 suggests moderate specialization, with food-related enterprises playing a particularly prominent role in the local economy. Community sentiment remains positive overall, while neighborhood conversations frequently center on local landmarks and infrastructure, from the Lincoln Park Conservancy and park district amenities to ongoing utility and development projects like the proposed ComEd work. These discussions reflect a community actively engaged with both the neighborhood's recreational assets and its evolving built environment.
