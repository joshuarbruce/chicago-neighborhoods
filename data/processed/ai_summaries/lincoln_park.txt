# Lincoln Park Neighborhood Profile

With 2,127 active business licenses concentrated heavily in food and beverage, health and personal care, Lincoln Park maintains a moderately diverse commercial ecosystem that caters to both residents and visitors. The neighborhood's 325 Airbnb listings command a median nightly rate of $206, suggesting strong tourist appeal centered on attractions like the Lincoln Park Zoo and easy Brown Line access. While residents discuss infrastructure quirks—hydrant parking, low-flying planes, and proposed utility changes—visitors emphasize walkability, dining options, and proximity to cultural amenities, indicating that tourism messaging focuses on convenience and leisure experiences that residents take for granted or navigate around daily. Overall community sentiment remains positive, though the neighborhood's commercial character is shaped more by transient visitor interests than the neighborhood-specific concerns that dominate resident conversations.
