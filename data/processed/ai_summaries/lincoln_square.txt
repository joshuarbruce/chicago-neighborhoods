# Lincoln Square

With 1,148 active business licenses and a moderate diversity index of 1.22, this neighborhood supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services. Residents engage actively with local institutions and civic issues—discussing community spaces like Homan Square and Square Roots, transit access, and neighborhood governance through social media forums. Airbnb visitors (median nightly rate: $114 across 123 listings) emphasize leisure amenities like Winnemac Park and the Brown Line's accessibility, suggesting that tourists are drawn primarily to the neighborhood's recreational and transit infrastructure, while long-term residents focus more on local civic engagement and community-building efforts.
