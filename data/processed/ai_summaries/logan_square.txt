# Logan Square

With 2,118 active business licenses, Logan Square supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.17 suggests a moderately concentrated economy, with these top categories dominating the commercial landscape. Community discussions highlight the area's social gathering spaces, including popular bars, cafes, and dining destinations, alongside transit accessibility via the Blue Line and neighborhood amenities like Palmer Square Park and the Logan Square Library. Overall community sentiment remains positive, reflecting resident engagement with the neighborhood's commercial and recreational offerings.
