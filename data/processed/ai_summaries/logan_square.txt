# Logan Square

With 2,118 active business licenses and a business diversity index of 1.17, the neighborhood supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food & beverage, automotive, and other service sectors. Residents highlight local gathering spots like Palmer Square Park, the Logan Square Library, and specific establishments like Cafe Mustache, emphasizing community connections and accessible transit via the Blue Line. Visitors consistently praise the neighborhood's walkable concentration of coffee shops, restaurants, and bars—particularly those near the Blue Line station—suggesting they prioritize convenience and nightlife options that residents mention less frequently. Overall community sentiment is positive across both groups.
