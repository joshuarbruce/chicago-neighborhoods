# Loop Neighborhood Profile

With 5,573 active business licenses spanning diverse sectors—led by food and beverage, health and personal care, and other services—the Loop functions as a densely commercial urban core. The neighborhood maintains a moderate business diversity index of 1.09, suggesting some concentration among dominant sectors rather than a highly varied economy. While Airbnb visitors emphasize scenic attractions like Millennium Park and Grant Park, along with hotel amenities and views, residents' Reddit discussions center on transit infrastructure, local venues, and civic matters, indicating that visitors prioritize the neighborhood's recreational and hospitality appeal while residents engage with its functionality as a working and living urban space. Overall community sentiment registers as positive across both visitor and resident voices.
