# Lower West Side, Chicago

With 1,487 active business licenses and a relatively low business diversity index of 1.06, the neighborhood's economy is concentrated in a few sectors, particularly Food & Beverage, Automotive & Transportation, and miscellaneous services. Residents engage with the area's established institutions and natural features, discussing neighborhood landmarks like Noble Square and Santa Maria Addolorata church alongside practical concerns like water pressure and flash flooding. Meanwhile, Airbnb visitors (256 active listings at a median nightly rate of $113) emphasize cultural attractions—the Mexican Art Museum, Thalia Hall, and local Mexican restaurants—and transit access via the Pink Line, suggesting that tourists are drawn to the neighborhood's artistic and cultural amenities more than residents highlight in their everyday discussions.
