# McKinley Park Neighborhood Profile

With 357 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial economy anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.16 indicates a relatively concentrated commercial landscape dominated by a few key sectors rather than a wide variety of business types. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood registers as positive overall, reflecting favorable resident perception. Online discussions highlight local amenities including park district facilities and the river system, while also touching on employment opportunities and broader city connections to areas like Lincoln Park and Arlington Heights.
