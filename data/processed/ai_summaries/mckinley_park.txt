# McKinley Park Neighborhood Summary

With 357 active business licenses concentrated in food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, McKinley Park supports a modest local economy that reflects moderate business diversity. Residents engage in conversations centered on community institutions like the park district and river system, alongside local priorities like workforce development and removing barriers to employment. Visitors to the neighborhood's 68 Airbnb listings—typically priced at $46 per night—emphasize proximity to transit infrastructure, particularly the CTA Orange Line, and nearby dining destinations like Casa Montoya, suggesting that accessibility and affordable dining options hold particular appeal for short-term guests in ways that long-term residents don't frequently highlight. Overall community sentiment about the neighborhood is positive.
