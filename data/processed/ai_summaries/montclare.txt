# Montclare Neighborhood Profile

With 269 active business licenses, Montclare supports a modest commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.21 indicates a relatively concentrated business landscape, suggesting that a few dominant sectors drive economic activity rather than a wide variety of industries. The prevalence of "Other" as the top business category alongside food service and transportation reflects a neighborhood commercial base oriented toward everyday services and local needs.
