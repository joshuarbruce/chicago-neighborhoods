# Morgan Park Neighborhood Profile

With 329 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial base anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.11 suggests a relatively concentrated economy with limited variety across sectors. Community sentiment remains positive overall, though Reddit discussions reveal infrastructure frustrations centered on snow removal effectiveness, bike lane maintenance, and street clearing practices that disproportionately affect main thoroughfares. These operational concerns coexist with residents' engagement in neighborhood discourse spanning adjacent areas like Chatham and Englewood.
