# Mount Greenwood

With 219 active business licenses concentrated in food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Mount Greenwood supports a modest commercial ecosystem, though its business diversity index of 1.12 suggests limited variety. Residents engage actively with infrastructure concerns, particularly around snow removal effectiveness and bike lane conditions on main streets, while also discussing parking and street maintenance issues—topics notably absent from the sparse visitor reviews of the neighborhood's two Airbnb listings. The few short-term rental guests who do stay emphasize the charm and comfort of individual properties, praising their coziness and accessibility, yet this hospitality angle remains largely tangential to how residents experience and discuss their community. Overall community sentiment registers as positive despite the infrastructure-focused complaints that dominate local conversations.
