# Near North Side, Chicago

With 6,391 active business licenses and a moderate diversity index of 1.38, Near North Side maintains a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage, health and personal care, and miscellaneous services. The neighborhood attracts significant tourism, with 1,139 Airbnb listings commanding a median nightly rate of $236, though visitor reviews focus heavily on upscale shopping districts and iconic architecture while residents engage more with civic concerns like infrastructure, schools, and quality-of-life issues. Community sentiment remains positive overall, reflecting both the neighborhood's appeal as a destination and residents' active engagement with local challenges. While Airbnb guests celebrate the area's world-class amenities and transit access, residents' conversations reveal deeper investment in neighborhood institutions and ongoing advocacy around service equity.
