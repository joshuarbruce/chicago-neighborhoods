# Near North Side Neighborhood Profile

With 6,391 active business licenses, Near North Side maintains a robust commercial landscape dominated by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.38 suggests a moderate concentration in specific sectors rather than a highly varied economy. Community sentiment remains positive overall, though Reddit discussions reveal residents engage with infrastructure concerns including transportation issues, service accessibility, and environmental factors like aircraft noise. The presence of notable landmarks such as Trump Tower and the river system, combined with mentions of organized community efforts and school clusters, indicates an active neighborhood with residents invested in local advocacy and civic participation.
