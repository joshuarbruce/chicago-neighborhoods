# Near South Side

With 658 active business licenses concentrated in food & beverage, health & personal care, and other services, Near South Side supports a moderately diverse local economy (Shannon entropy: 1.25). Residents engage actively around practical neighborhood concerns—school quality, food security, mutual aid networks, and infrastructure issues—while the 278 Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $213 suggest visitors are primarily drawn to the neighborhood's proximity to major venues like McCormick Center and Soldier Field rather than the residential community life that dominates local conversations. The contrast between visitor and resident focus reveals a neighborhood functioning simultaneously as both a residential community addressing everyday needs and a hospitality hub for convention and sports tourism. Overall community sentiment remains positive despite these distinct priorities.
