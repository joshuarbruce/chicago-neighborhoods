# Near West Side Neighborhood Profile

With 3,130 active business licenses and a business diversity index of 1.25, Near West Side supports a moderately varied economic ecosystem anchored by food and beverage, automotive, and other service sectors. The neighborhood hosts 686 active Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $199, reflecting moderate tourism activity. Residents engage enthusiastically with local destinations like Fulton Market and Bartelme Park, while also discussing practical concerns like car washes and community amenities such as art classes, suggesting a neighborhood balancing commercial vitality with quality-of-life priorities. Notably, visitors' reviews emphasize transit accessibility and institutional anchors—the Blue, Pink, and Green lines, the United Center, UIC campus, and the Medical District—highlighting connectivity and destination proximity that residents mention less frequently in their discussions.
