# Near West Side Neighborhood Profile

With 3,130 active business licenses, this neighborhood maintains a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments, automotive services, and miscellaneous enterprises. The business diversity index of 1.25 suggests a moderately concentrated economy, indicating these top categories dominate the commercial landscape. Community discussions frequently reference landmarks like Fulton Market and Bartelme Park alongside everyday activities such as car washing and art classes, reflecting both recreational amenities and practical services. Overall community sentiment remains positive, suggesting residents engage constructively with their neighborhood environment.
