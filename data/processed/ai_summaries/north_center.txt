# North Center, Chicago

With 1,168 active business licenses concentrated in food & beverage, health services, and miscellaneous retail, North Center maintains a moderately diverse commercial ecosystem. Residents engage with neighborhood discussions centered on practical concerns—utility costs, income levels, and infrastructure—alongside cultural touchstones like Cubs games and the nearby holiday train displays. Visitors, meanwhile, emphasize the neighborhood's walkability, proximity to Wrigley Field and Lincoln Square, and abundance of restaurants and coffee shops, suggesting that tourists value the area's leisure and entertainment appeal in ways that don't prominently surface in resident conversations. The 144 active Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $156 indicate the neighborhood has become a notable destination for short-term visitors attracted to its transit access and recreational attractions.
