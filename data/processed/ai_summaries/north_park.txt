# North Park Neighborhood Profile

With 452 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a moderately diverse economy anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.23 indicates a relatively concentrated commercial landscape, suggesting these core sectors dominate economic activity. Community discussions on Reddit reveal significant engagement with local infrastructure and amenities, particularly around water systems, park district services, and parking availability. Overall sentiment in the neighborhood registers as positive, reflecting general community satisfaction despite practical concerns about transportation and municipal services.
