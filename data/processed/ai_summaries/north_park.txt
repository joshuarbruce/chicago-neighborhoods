# North Park Neighborhood Profile

With 452 active business licenses and a moderate business diversity index of 1.23, North Park's economy centers on food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services. Residents engage most with discussions about infrastructure and local institutions—the park district, river systems, and nearby landmarks like Trump Tower—suggesting these are defining features of daily life in the neighborhood. Visitors to North Park's 22 Airbnb listings (median $94/night) emphasize personal travel experiences and local dining destinations like Bryn Mawr Breakfast Club, whereas residents' online conversations rarely highlight food culture, indicating that visitors discover and value the neighborhood's culinary scene more prominently than longtime residents discuss it. Overall community sentiment in North Park registers as positive.
