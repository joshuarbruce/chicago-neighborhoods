# Oakland Neighborhood Profile

With 42 active business licenses, Oakland maintains a modest commercial presence centered on food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.16 indicates relatively concentrated economic activity rather than a broad range of business types. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood remains positive overall. Online discussions about Oakland prominently feature topics related to military, law enforcement, immigration policy, and federal governance, suggesting these are significant issues shaping public discourse in the area.
