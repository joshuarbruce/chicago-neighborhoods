# O'Hare Neighborhood Profile

With 488 active business licenses and a business diversity index of 1.18, O'Hare maintains a relatively concentrated economy anchored by food and beverage, health and personal care, and other service businesses. Community sentiment remains positive, though resident discussions center heavily on airport operations, airline logistics, and government regulatory matters affecting the area. Visitors booking the neighborhood's 12 Airbnb listings (median nightly rate: $112) emphasize practical amenities like airport access, nearby grocers, and the Allstate Arena—convenience-oriented features that residents rarely highlight in their online conversations, suggesting guests prioritize proximity and functionality while residents engage more with the institutional and operational dimensions of living near a major transportation hub.
