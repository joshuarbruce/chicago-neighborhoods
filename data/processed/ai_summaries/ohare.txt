# O'Hare Neighborhood Profile

With 488 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The business diversity index of 1.18 indicates a relatively concentrated economic base, suggesting these few dominant sectors drive much of the commercial activity. Online discussions center heavily on airport operations, airline industry developments, and government regulatory matters, reflecting the neighborhood's deep connection to aviation infrastructure. Despite the specialized economic focus, community sentiment remains positive.
