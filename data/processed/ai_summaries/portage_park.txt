# Portage Park

With 1,386 active business licenses and a moderate business diversity index of 1.13, Portage Park maintains a robust commercial landscape anchored by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services. Residents engage actively with local institutions like the park district and foundry, while discussing recreational destinations across the broader region including Lincoln Park and the Chicago River system. Airbnb visitors, paying a median of $102 nightly across 121 listings, emphasize proximity to transit infrastructure—particularly the Blue Line station and Jefferson Park station—suggesting that transportation access and convenient connections to other neighborhoods represent a key draw for short-term visitors that doesn't feature prominently in resident conversations. The neighborhood's positive community sentiment reflects both active local business investment and appeal to transient visitors seeking accessible urban lodging.
