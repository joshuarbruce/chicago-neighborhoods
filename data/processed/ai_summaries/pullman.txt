# Pullman

With 134 active business licenses, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial footprint anchored primarily by food and beverage establishments, alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.08 indicates relatively concentrated economic activity rather than a broad range of commercial sectors. Community sentiment toward Pullman registers as positive overall, though Reddit discussions reveal mixed perspectives—ranging from appreciation for local dining and cultural attractions like the Pullman Porter Museum to concerns about neighborhood disadvantages. The neighborhood's character emerges through references to both its culinary identity and historical significance, balanced against acknowledgment of infrastructure and development challenges.
