# Pullman Neighborhood Profile

With 134 active business licenses anchored primarily in food and beverage, automotive, and other sectors, Pullman maintains modest economic activity, though its business diversity index of 1.08 suggests a relatively concentrated commercial landscape. Residents engage enthusiastically with the neighborhood's cultural anchors—the Pullman Porter Museum and the historic district—while also acknowledging infrastructure challenges, according to Reddit discussions that reflect overall positive community sentiment. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, emphasize the neighborhood's artistic identity and historic preservation appeal, frequently referencing artist residencies, art studios, and the national monument designation; this focus on creative and heritage tourism appears more prominent in visitor commentary than in resident conversations, which center more on local dining and civic concerns.
