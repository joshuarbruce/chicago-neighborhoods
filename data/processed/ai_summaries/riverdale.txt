# Riverdale Neighborhood Profile

With 44 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial base anchored by automotive, transportation, and food and beverage establishments, alongside miscellaneous other ventures. The business diversity index of 0.81 suggests a moderately concentrated economy rather than a broadly varied commercial landscape. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood trends positive, and local discussions frequently reference transportation infrastructure like the Dan Ryan Expressway and Red Line, as well as architectural features including courtyard buildings, indicating these elements play a notable role in residents' perceptions of the area.
