# Riverdale Neighborhood Profile

With 44 active business licenses concentrated primarily in automotive, transportation, and food and beverage sectors, Riverdale maintains a relatively focused economic footprint with low business diversity (0.81 Shannon entropy). Residents emphasize the neighborhood's architectural character, particularly courtyard-style buildings, and frequently reference its southwest side location and proximity to the Dan Ryan Expressway and Red Line transit. Despite limited commercial variety, the neighborhood generates positive community sentiment among those who engage in local discussions. The absence of Airbnb listings suggests minimal short-term rental activity, indicating Riverdale functions primarily as a residential neighborhood rather than a visitor destination.
