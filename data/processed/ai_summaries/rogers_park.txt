# Rogers Park Neighborhood Profile

With 1,170 active business licenses spanning food and beverage, retail, and other sectors, Rogers Park supports a moderately diverse commercial landscape (diversity index: 1.2). The neighborhood draws 187 Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $102, with visitors frequently mentioning proximity to Loyola University and Lake Michigan. Residents engage actively on social media discussing local landmarks like Loyola Beach and the park district, alongside quirks like urban coyotes and helicopter traffic. A notable contrast emerges between visitor and resident perspectives: guests emphasize the neighborhood's waterfront and university proximity, while residents more frequently celebrate cultural assets like its art hub identity and distinctive local establishments.
