# Roseland Neighborhood Profile

With 603 active business licenses concentrated in food and beverage, retail, and miscellaneous services, Roseland supports a moderately diverse local economy. Residents engage primarily with practical neighborhood concerns—parking accessibility, proximity to Soldier Field, and navigating local systems—suggesting these are central to daily life in the area. Airbnb visitors, by contrast, emphasize residential amenities like hot tubs and massage chairs, valuing the neighborhood as a vacation rental destination in ways that don't appear in resident conversations. Overall community sentiment remains positive despite the logistical friction residents frequently discuss.
