# South Shore Neighborhood Profile

With 777 active business licenses, South Shore supports a modest commercial presence anchored primarily by food and beverage establishments and retail operations. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.14 suggests a relatively concentrated economic base, with limited variety in business types. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood registers as positive, while online discussions reflect resident engagement around local infrastructure concerns—particularly water pressure and shoreline issues—alongside community organizing efforts around food security and mutual aid initiatives.
