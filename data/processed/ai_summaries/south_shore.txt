# South Shore

With 777 active business licenses across food and beverage, retail, and other sectors, South Shore supports a moderately diverse local economy (diversity index: 1.14). Residents engage in community-focused conversations centered on food security, mutual aid groups, and neighborhood infrastructure like water pressure and the ice raid building. Visitors to the neighborhood's 133 Airbnb listings ($105 median nightly rate) emphasize proximity to amenities like Rainbow Beach, Jackson Park, and Lake Michigan access—natural and recreational assets that rarely surface in resident discussions, suggesting tourists are drawn primarily to the area's waterfront and park attractions rather than its commercial or social fabric.
