# Uptown Neighborhood Profile

With 1,362 active business licenses, Uptown supports a diverse commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside health and personal care services. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.2 suggests a moderately varied economic landscape beyond these dominant categories. Online discussions highlight community engagement around local venues like Uptown Taproom and Femme Stand, as well as proximity to recreational spaces such as Buena Park, reflecting residents' investment in neighborhood amenities. Overall community sentiment toward the neighborhood remains positive.
