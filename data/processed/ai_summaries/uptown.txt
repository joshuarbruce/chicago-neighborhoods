# Uptown, Chicago

With 1,362 active business licenses and a moderate business diversity index of 1.2, Uptown supports a substantial local economy anchored by food & beverage, health & personal care, and miscellaneous services. Residents engage actively with neighborhood landmarks like Buena Park and local establishments such as Uptown Taproom, while also discussing nearby areas like Andersonville and Edgewater, suggesting a community invested in the broader North Side corridor. Visitors, by contrast, emphasize entertainment venues—the Aragon Ballroom, Green Mill, and Riviera Theater—and proximity to major attractions like Wrigley Field and Montrose Beach, highlighting cultural and recreational destinations that appeal to tourists but receive less attention in resident conversations.
