# Washington Heights, Chicago

With 314 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial base anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. The business diversity index of 1.27 suggests a relatively concentrated economy with limited variety across sectors. Community sentiment remains positive, though online discussions reveal significant engagement with immigration policy, law enforcement, and local leadership issues. The neighborhood's commercial landscape reflects a practical, service-oriented economy serving local residents' everyday needs.
