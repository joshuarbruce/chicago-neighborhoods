# Washington Park

With 139 active business licenses concentrated heavily in food and beverage, automotive, and other services, Washington Park supports a modest commercial ecosystem with moderate diversity. The neighborhood maintains positive community sentiment, though residents' Reddit discussions center on civic issues like immigration reform and park district matters, while Airbnb visitors emphasize cultural attractions—particularly art collections and proximity to transit like the Green Line and Garfield station. The $126 median nightly Airbnb rate suggests the neighborhood appeals to tourists seeking accessible accommodation near cultural destinations, a draw that neighborhood residents themselves rarely highlight in their own conversations.
