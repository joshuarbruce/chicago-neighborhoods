# West Englewood

With 445 active business licenses, West Englewood supports a modest commercial base dominated by food and beverage establishments, automotive and transportation services, and miscellaneous other businesses. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.23 indicates a relatively concentrated economic landscape, suggesting that a few business categories account for a significant share of commercial activity rather than a broad range of industries. The prevalence of food service and transportation-related businesses points to establishments serving local daily needs.
