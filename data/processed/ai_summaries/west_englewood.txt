# West Englewood

The neighborhood supports 445 active businesses, with a modest diversity index of 1.23, concentrated primarily in food and beverage, automotive and transportation, and miscellaneous services. A small but established short-term rental market operates here, with 21 active Airbnb listings averaging $72 per night. Visitor reviews suggest guests particularly value affordable accommodations and specific amenities like recreational features, though the limited and fragmented guest commentary makes broader patterns difficult to discern. Without resident data available, the neighborhood's business activity and visitor accommodations remain the primary data-driven indicators of local economic activity.
