# West Garfield Park

With 375 active business licenses spanning food and beverage, retail, and other sectors, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial ecosystem, though its business diversity index of 1.15 suggests relatively concentrated economic activity. Residents engage around local infrastructure like Divvy stations and rail crossings, alongside community-building activities such as fundraisers and Earth Day events, reflecting a positive overall community sentiment. Airbnb visitors—drawn by the median nightly rate of $74 across 22 listings—emphasize access to transit hubs like the Blue Line station and cultural attractions like Garfield Park Conservatory, amenities that residents tend to take for granted in their neighborhood discussions.
