# West Garfield Park

With 375 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a modest commercial foundation anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside retail and miscellaneous services. The business diversity index of 1.15 suggests a relatively concentrated economic base without significant variety across sectors. Community sentiment remains positive overall, though Reddit discussions reveal concerns about infrastructure safety—particularly rail crossings and pedestrian incidents—alongside community engagement through local initiatives like fundraisers, Earth Day events, and recreational amenities including a skate park and Divvy bike station. The neighborhood's commercial and civic landscape reflects both economic activity and ongoing attention to public safety and quality-of-life improvements.
