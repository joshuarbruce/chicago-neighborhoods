# West Lawn Neighborhood Profile

With 662 active business licenses, the neighborhood supports a robust commercial foundation anchored by food and beverage, automotive and transportation, and miscellaneous service businesses. The business diversity index of 0.96 indicates moderate economic specialization, suggesting some concentration in particular sectors while maintaining variety across different industries. Community sentiment remains positive, reflecting constructive engagement among residents. Online discussions highlight cultural and community-oriented interests, including seasonal attractions, local institutions like the Days Art Institute, and civic participation around events like Earth Day.
