# West Lawn Neighborhood Profile

With 662 active business licenses and a moderate business diversity index of 0.96, West Lawn maintains a solid commercial foundation anchored by food and beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services. Residents highlight the neighborhood's cultural and community institutions—from the Santa Maria Addolorata church to the Days Art Institute—suggesting a tight-knit community engaged with local events and traditions like holiday celebrations and Earth Day activities. The 15 Airbnb listings (median $101/night) draw visitors primarily for airport proximity, with guest reviews repeatedly praising a host named Erik and the Zuniga family's hospitality; notably, visitors emphasize personal service and friendliness in ways that don't appear in resident discussions, suggesting short-term visitors value interpersonal connections and hosts who facilitate their travel logistics more than long-term community members do.
