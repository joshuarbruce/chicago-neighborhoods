# West Pullman Neighborhood Profile

With 311 active business licenses, the neighborhood maintains a modest commercial presence anchored by food and beverage, automotive and transportation, and miscellaneous services. The business diversity index of 1.21 indicates a relatively concentrated economy with limited variety across sectors. Community sentiment remains positive, and local discussions reflect engagement with cultural institutions like the Days Art Institute, seasonal events including holiday train displays, and neighborhood landmarks such as Santa Maria Addolorata and the Central Manufacturing District. The neighborhood demonstrates active community participation around free cultural programming and local environmental initiatives like Earth Day.
