# West Ridge Neighborhood Profile

West Ridge supports a moderately diverse business ecosystem of 1,482 active licenses, anchored by food and beverage establishments alongside automotive and transportation services. Residents engage with the neighborhood through civic and recreational activities—citing Ridge Nature Park, Earth Day events, and seasonal attractions like holiday trains—while visitors gravitate toward the Devon Avenue corridor's Indian dining scene and transit-adjacent amenities near the Howard Red Line station. Notably, Airbnb guests emphasize food experiences (free breakfast, specific restaurants, Indian cuisine) more prominently than Reddit residents, who focus instead on community events and natural spaces. The neighborhood maintains a positive community sentiment despite its relatively modest business diversity index.
