# West Town Neighborhood Profile

With 3,119 active business licenses, West Town supports a robust commercial ecosystem anchored by food and beverage establishments, health and personal care services, and other miscellaneous businesses. The neighborhood's business diversity index of 1.23 suggests a moderately concentrated business landscape, indicating that while multiple sectors operate here, a few categories dominate the economic activity. Community sentiment toward the neighborhood is positive, and online discussions frequently reference local dining and entertainment venues—including Club Lucky, Big Star, and Van Leeuwen—alongside mentions of the rental market and neighborhood identity markers like the Polish Triangle and Wicker Park areas. This profile reflects a vibrant, commercially active neighborhood with strong food culture and residential interest.
