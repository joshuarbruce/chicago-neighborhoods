# Woodlawn

With 336 active business licenses concentrated in food & beverage, automotive, and miscellaneous services, Woodlawn supports a modest but functional commercial ecosystem. The neighborhood attracts significant short-term rental activity, with 234 Airbnb listings at a median nightly rate of $85, drawing visitors primarily interested in proximity to the University of Chicago campus and nearby transit hubs like the Green Line and Cottage Grove station. While Airbnb reviews emphasize the neighborhood's connectivity to educational and recreational destinations—Hyde Park, Washington Park, and local amenities—resident discussions on Reddit focus more on housing affordability challenges and quality-of-life concerns, suggesting visitors and long-term residents experience the neighborhood quite differently. Overall community sentiment registers as positive despite these underlying tensions.
